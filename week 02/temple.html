    Aba Niger Temple

    About the temple

    The Aba Temple, Nigeria, is a temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, the 121st in operation, dedicated on August 7, 2005, by President Gordon B. Hinckley. It serves members in Nigeria and Cameroon and features African-inspired architecture and design, with hardwood furniture and brightly colored fabrics.
